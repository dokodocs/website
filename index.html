Features

Everything a scanner should do — nothing it shouldn't.

DokoDocs is built around one idea: your documents are yours. Every feature respects that.

Smart Scanning

Auto edge detection, manual crop, and perspective correction get a clean scan every time.

Enhance & Filter

Grayscale, black & white, and enhanced-contrast filters make documents sharp and readable.

PDF Toolkit

Local First, Always

A closer look

Made for the way you actually keep documents.

Scan any document instantly

Point, capture, done. Edge detection finds the page for you, and manual crop is always one tap away when you need it.

  • Auto edge detection with manual override
  • Perspective correction for angled shots
  • Multi-page capture in one session

Stay organized, automatically

Every scan lands somewhere sensible. Folders, tags, and favorites keep things findable without extra effort.

  • Folders and tags for fast filtering
  • Search across your whole library
  • Favorites for the documents you reach for most

Most scanner apps quietly upload your documents. This one doesn't.

DokoDocs works differently: your documents stay on your device unless you choose to sync them — to your own server, your own cloud, or nowhere at all. It's open-source, so you can verify that for yourself instead of taking our word for it.
