### Day 4 — Record the proof-of-product demo

**Shot list (one continuous 30–35 second screen recording):**
1. (0:00–0:04) Open app → tap Scan
2. (0:04–0:10) Camera auto-detects a document edge → capture
3. (0:10–0:15) Manual crop adjustment (drag one corner slightly, to show it's possible)
4. (0:15–0:20) Apply a filter (tap through Original → B&W → Enhance)
5. (0:20–0:25) Add a second page, show the multi-page reorder screen
6. (0:25–0:30) Tap "Save as PDF" → PDF preview appears
7. (0:30–0:35) Tap Share → native share sheet opens

Record at 1080p portrait, no narration needed — add on-screen captions instead (cheaper to produce, works muted on social feeds where most people watch). Suggested captions: *"Scan →"* *"Auto-detect edge"* *"Crop & enhance"* *"Combine to PDF"* *"Share — no account needed."*

Tool: use your phone's built-in screen recorder — no extra software needed.
