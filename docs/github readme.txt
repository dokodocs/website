## What is DokoDocs?

DokoDocs scans, enhances, and exports documents to PDF — with your files staying **on your device by default**. No forced cloud upload, no mandatory account. When you do want to sync, you choose the destination: your own server, your own cloud provider, or nowhere at all.

Built in Nepal 🇳🇵, open-source under Apache 2.0.

## Features

- 📷 Auto edge detection + manual crop & perspective correction
- 🎨 Filters: Original, Grayscale, Black & White, Enhanced contrast
- 📑 Multi-page scanning, reorder, combine into a single PDF
- 📤 Instant share — no account required
- 🌐 Optional sync to your own server or cloud storage
- 🔒 Local-first storage by default
